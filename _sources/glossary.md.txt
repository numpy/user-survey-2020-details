# Glossary

```{glossary}

Community
   The NumPy community comprises everyone who uses NumPy, regardless of 
   activity.

Location
   Country information for users is based on the responses submitted by
   the survey participants

NumPy users
   Anyone who uses NumPy, regardless of their activity, and participated
   in this survey.

Open source projects
   Opensource projects are public repositories with an open source
   license.

```
