NumPy Community Survey Results - 2020¶

Welcome!¶

In mid-2020, the NumPy Survey Team in partnership with students and faculty from a Masters course in Survey Methodology jointly hosted by the University of Michigan and the Unverisity of Maryland conducted the first official NumPy Community Survey. We invited all NumPy stakeholders to share their experiences with NumPy, and to voice their thoughts about the future of the project. Over 1,200 users from 75 countries participated to help us map out a landscape of the NumPy community.
